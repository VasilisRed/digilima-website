Project Overview

Rhodes Boutique Hotel is a luxury 5-star property located in the historic center of Rhodes Old Town. They needed a website that would reflect their premium positioning while providing seamless online booking capabilities to reduce dependency on third-party booking platforms.

Business Challenges

  • High Commission Fees: 85% of bookings came through OTAs with 15-20% commission fees
  • Brand Disconnection: Existing website didn't reflect the hotel's luxury positioning
  • Poor Mobile Experience: Most potential guests browsed on mobile but couldn't complete bookings
  • Limited Direct Marketing: No way to build guest relationships for repeat business

Design Philosophy

We created a website that embodies the spirit of Greek hospitality while showcasing the hotel's unique character:

Visual Storytelling

High-quality photography and immersive galleries that transport visitors to the Greek islands.

Seamless Booking

Integrated booking engine with real-time availability and special offers for direct bookings.

Local Experience

Interactive maps and recommendations that help guests discover Rhodes beyond the hotel.

Guest Portal

Pre-arrival services and personalized recommendations based on guest preferences.
