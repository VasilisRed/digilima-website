Project Overview

Paphos Law Firm is a well-established legal practice specializing in immigration law, property law, and corporate services for international clients in Cyprus. They needed a professional website that would build trust with potential clients while providing secure communication channels.

Business Requirements

  • Professional Image: Website needed to reflect the firm's expertise and reliability
  • Client Portal: Secure area for clients to access documents and case updates
  • Online Booking: System for scheduling consultations and appointments
  • Multilingual Support: Content in English, Greek, and Russian for international clients
  • GDPR Compliance: Strict data protection requirements for legal practice
