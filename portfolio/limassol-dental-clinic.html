Project Overview

The Limassol Dental Clinic approached us with an outdated website that was not mobile-friendly and difficult for patients to navigate. Their main goals were to increase online visibility, improve patient experience, and streamline the appointment booking process.

Challenges

  • Poor Mobile Experience: The old site was not responsive and difficult to use on mobile devices
  • Low Search Rankings: Poor SEO performance and limited organic traffic
  • Complex Navigation: Patients struggled to find service information and contact details
  • No Online Booking: All appointments required phone calls, creating friction

Solution

We designed and developed a modern, patient-focused website with the following key features:

Mobile-First Design

Responsive design optimized for all devices, ensuring excellent user experience on mobile and desktop.

SEO Optimization

Comprehensive SEO strategy including local search optimization, structured data, and content optimization.

Online Booking System

Integrated appointment booking system allowing patients to schedule visits directly through the website.

Patient Portal

Secure patient portal for accessing medical records, treatment plans, and appointment history.
