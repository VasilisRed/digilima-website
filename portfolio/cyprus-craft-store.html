Project Overview

Cyprus Craft Store is an online marketplace featuring handmade products from local Cypriot artisans. The store was struggling with slow loading times, poor mobile experience, and low conversion rates despite having quality products and good traffic.

E-commerce Challenges

  • Poor Performance: Store was loading in 6+ seconds, causing high bounce rates
  • Mobile Issues: Checkout process was difficult on mobile devices (70% of traffic)
  • Low Conversions: Only 1.2% conversion rate despite good product quality
  • Navigation Problems: Customers couldn't easily find products or categories
  • Trust Issues: Lack of social proof and security indicators

Optimization Strategy

We implemented a comprehensive optimization strategy focused on performance, UX, and conversions:

Performance Optimization

Custom theme optimization, image compression, and code minification for faster loading.

Mobile-First Redesign

Completely rebuilt mobile checkout flow and product browsing experience.

Conversion Optimization

A/B tested product pages, checkout flow, and added trust signals throughout the store.

SEO Enhancement

Optimized product descriptions, meta tags, and structured data for better search visibility.
